About the Drive

Optifocal drive is a computer vision application aimed acurately identifying a driver and then proctoring their attention to ensure that they stay alert and focused. By using OpenCV, Dlib, PnP (perspective N point) and SciPy our model is capable of accurately identifying a single face out of many, monitoring their attention, and then taking action when their attention has {drifted}.
